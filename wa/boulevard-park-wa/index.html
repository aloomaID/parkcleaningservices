Boulevard Park, WA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Boulevard Park is located at 47°30′44″N 122°18′47″W (47.512172, -122.313151). It is bordered to the north by Seattle, to the east by Tukwila, to the south by Burien, and to the west by the White Center CDP.
