Cloquet, MN Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Cloquet (/kloʊˈkeɪ/ i kloh-KAY) is a city in Carlton County, Minnesota, United States, at the junction of Interstate 35 and Minnesota State Highway 33. Part of the city lies within the Fond du Lac Indian Reservation and serves as one of the reservation's three administrative centers. The population was 12,568 at the 2020 census.
